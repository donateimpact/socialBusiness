Problem

Plenty of engaged people struggle to finance their projects effectively. Equally, various beneficent people strive to support initiatives purposefully. Organizations mediating the donation process cost money and time, but each, donatee and benefactor, are unwilling to lose value by resigning on these intermediaries.

Our approach

​The DonateImpact Initiative ensures the security and traceability of each donation. Based on the Ethereum Blockchain, we enable donatees to raise their projects autonomously and donators to invest into them straightforwardly. By incarnating a project, the donor recipients simultaneously trigger the embodiment of a smart contract, which allows the benefactors to process the donation directly to the project – without any intermediating institution. ​

​ Those who are interested in our society’s progress can donate directly to those who have the time and passion for engaging. Today, costly organizations linking both sides are becoming obsolete. By means of latest technology, the process of transferring the donation from the benefactor to the donatee can be executed as fast and efficient as possible.
